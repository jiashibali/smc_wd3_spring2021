Meatless mincemeat

Despite its name, our delicious pie filling actually contains no meat. Perhaps it did incorporate bits of animal flesh in ancient times, but today mincemeat is a mix of apple, raisins and other fruits (fresh and dried), candied peel, vegetable shortening and mixed spices.
